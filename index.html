Reviewer Scoring Instrument

This study asks how you see the woman in a song lyric.

For each song you will read the lyric, answer a few questions about how it made you feel, and describe how you saw the woman in it.

There are no right or wrong answers. This is a study of perception, not analysis.

The study takes about two minutes per song. You can stop at any time.
